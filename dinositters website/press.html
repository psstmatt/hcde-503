DinoSitters Club, a Modern, Urban Babysitting Service, Launches in Seattle

A new service

A team of students at the University of Washington today announced the launch of DinoSitters Club, a service targeted at urban Seattle families looking for childcare providers. Residents of King County (in particular, Seattle, Bellevue, Redmond, and Kirkland) can now enlist the services of trained babysitters in a way that solves time and money.

“Research by developmental psychologists has shown that when children are among other children they don’t know well, they are much more cooperative, social, and empathetic” said Dr. T. Rax, an advisor to the DinoSitters Club, “we’re utilizing modern-day science to provide a stress-free, worry-free, and cost-effective babysitting service to urban families.”

The brand is named for the dinosaur hats that its babysitters wear when babysitting to make it easy for children to transition to trusting new faces should different DinoSitters be engaged by the same family at various times; DinoSitters Club assures families that although each DinoSitter is unique and different, the quality of service provided by any single DinoSitter will always be the same excellent quality that can be expected.

As DinoSitters are trained to take care of multiple children, families are encouraged to utilize the CostShare feature that allows two families to engage the same DinoSitter for the same duration at a cheaper price than engaging a dedicated DinoSitter each. This revolutionary concept follows the modern-day trends in the “sharing economy”. DinoSitters Club also embraces modern-day technology, touting a sensor-laden yet comfortable wristband each child will be asked to wear, which reports vital and location information back to the child’s parents; sparing parents the stress associated with leaving their children at home with a babysitter.

Diana Zore, a Seattle mom of 2 involved in the limited early preview program, offered the following review: “Before my neighbor told me about DinoSitters Club, having a date night was a luxury. Thanks to DinoSitters Club, now my husband and I can fully enjoy our dates! The Club truly saved our marriage! Funny story, our son won’t sleep now unless we put on a dinosaur hat just like his DinoSitter and read to him… you’ve found a little big fan!”

Learn more about DinoSitters Club and join the club at www.dinositters.club.
